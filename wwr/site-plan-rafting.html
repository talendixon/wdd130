Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide comprehensive information about rafting experiences, safety tips, and guides for all levels. It aims to inspire and prepare adventurers for their rafting journey.

Audience

The target audience includes adventure enthusiasts, families looking for fun activities, and individuals seeking adrenaline-pumping experiences. Our goal is to cater to both beginners and experienced rafters.

Fonts

The main font for headings will be 'Georgia', and the body text will be in 'Arial'. This combination provides a clear and appealing aesthetic.

Navigation

The navigation bar will be styled with a contrasting background color and will include links to all main sections of the site, making it easy for users to find information.

Site Map

The site will include the following pages:

  • Home
  • About Us
  • Rafting Packages
  • Safety Tips
  • Contact Us
  • Frequently Asked Questions

We will replace any future placeholders with more meaningful names as we finalize the site's content.

Wireframes

The wireframe shows what the home page will look like, detailing the layout and design elements. This wireframe will guide the development of the home page, and a wireframe for the child page will be created later in the semester.
